Coconut Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Coconut shreds
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • Milk - you will fill up the empty evaporated milk can with this for measurement
  • Vanilla or coconut pudding
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Butter
  • Your favorite pie crust

Directions

  1. Prepare pie crust based on package instructions
  2. Put evaporated milk, other milk thing, and pudding into a pot
  3. Continuously stir until it is halfway there
  4. Pour some mixture into yolks while beating them (gradually)
  5. Pour back into pie filling and continuously stir until thick
  6. Add some butter
  7. Fill sink to level that filling is in the pot with cold water (not ice water), put pot into the sink.
  8. Add coconut shreds and stir in
  9. Once cool enough pour into pie crust and refriderate
  10. Add whipped cream and serve cold
